System Pentester Roadmap

A structured 6-7 months plan to become a System Pentester

1. Computer & Networking Fundamentals

Duration: 1-1.5 Months

Description: Build a strong foundation in computer hardware, operating systems, networking basics, and programming/scripting.

Topics:

  • Computer Hardware & OS Internals
  • Operating Systems: Windows, Linux
  • Networking: TCP/IP, Subnetting, CIDR, OSI Model
  • Packet Analysis with Wireshark
  • Programming & Scripting: Python, Bash, C/C++

Resources:

  • CompTIA A+ (Udemy/ITPro)
  • Windows Internals (Pluralsight)
  • Linux+ (Udemy/ITPro)
  • Linux 101 (TCM Security)
  • Linux Journey / Hackersploit Linux Series
  • CompTIA Network+
  • Cisco Packet Tracer

2. Ethical Hacking Fundamentals

Duration: 1-2 Months

Description: Gain hands-on experience with essential hacking tools and techniques, focusing on system exploitation and privilege escalation.

Topics:

  • Hacking Tools: Wireshark, Nmap, Metasploit, Aircrack-ng, Netcat
  • Exploit Development & System Hacking
  • Privilege Escalation (Windows & Linux)

Resources:

  • TCM Security Buffer Overflow Course
  • Hack The Box (OS Exploitation & Privilege Escalation)
  • TryHackMe (Windows/Linux Pentesting Labs)
  • OverTheWire (Linux CTF Practice)

3. Specialization & Certifications

Duration: 2-3 Months

Description: Validate your skills with certifications and deepen your knowledge in system pentesting techniques.

Topics:

  • Beginner-Level: CompTIA Security+
  • Intermediate-Level: CEH, CompTIA PenTest+
  • Advanced-Level: OSCP, CRTP (Windows AD Hacking)

4. Boost Skills & Get Job-Ready

Duration: Ongoing

Description: Continue learning through real-world practice, labs, and CTF challenges.

Topics:

  • CTF Challenges (HTB, TryHackMe, CTFtime)
  • Home Pentesting Lab: Kali Linux, Windows VM, AD Lab Setup
  • Practice: Privilege Escalation & Lateral Movement
  • Advanced Tools: Mimikatz, BloodHound, Cobalt Strike
